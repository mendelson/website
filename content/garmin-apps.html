How do I buy a paid app?

Currently, we offer two payment systems: Garmin's and KiezelPay's.

  • Garmin:
    • We are currently working on making the apps available for purchase directly from Garmin's payment system. Check the links for each app. The one that uses Garmin's payment processor is the one popularly known as "paywall". You can only use it if you pay in advance. For this reason, I advise you to first test the app using the other links. Can't you find a paywall in any of the links for the app you want? Than it's either a free app or Garmin's processor is not available for that app.
  • KiezelPay:
    • Access mmendelson.com/garmin-pricing and choose the app(s) you want; or
    • Wait for the free trial to expire and follow the instructions that will appear on your watch's screen.

All the apps are one-time payments, which means that you only have to buy once and will have lifetime access to it.

Beware that I am not the one to handle reimbursements. I do not have control over it, but please reach out to me in case you have any difficulties.

Which payment system should I choose?

No matter the payment system you choose, you'll get the same base app. Different customizations or functionalities are due to different watch models' capabilities, mainly memory limitations. Using Garmin's payment system usually allows for more available memory, because their paywall is not handled inside the code itself. The bad side on that is the impossibility of free trials. On the other hand, KiezelPay takes some memory to handle the licensing, so some watches may not be able to support full functionalities. That's why there are "Lite" versions: they have less customizations and functionalities, but their main functionalities are still there. The good thing is that you can download the app and enjoy a free trial before deciding on your purchase.

The reason for adopting more than one payment system is to get a larger variety of payment options for those interested in buying the apps.

TL;DR: use free trial from KiezelPay, than try to purchase from Garmin's paywall. Need more payment options? Go with KiezelPay. Need even more payment options? Send me an e-mail.

How do I know which payment system I'm looking at?

To make things easier, just look at the download button. If it has a "free" tag, than it is a free app. If it has the "free trial" tag, than it is KiezelPay. If it doesn't have a tag, than it is Garmin's payment system.

I have already purchased the app on KiezelPay, but it is asking me to pay again.

If your watch shows the KiezelPay screen requesting you to go to the purchase website even after you have already made the purchase, just go to the website, click on "already purchased", input the same e-mail you used when you first bought the app and follow the instructions. This should unlock your app again without the need for another payment.
